Welcome to Department of CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DCSE) was established in 2002 and it is one of the two oldest departments of the university. The department was established with a vision to engender competent and skilled CSE graduates to avail and fill the lucrative job opportunities at that time. In spite of resource constraints at its early stage, care is taken so that high teaching quality is maintained at each stage of education. Premier University has established Cisco Networking Academy in collaboration with Cisco Systems where students and officials can learn professionalism.
